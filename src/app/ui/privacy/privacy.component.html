Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect, store, or upload any personal data or resumes/CVs you submit. The only information that is processed is the text extracted from the uploaded document, which is used solely to generate a score for the provided resume. We do not save, store, or retain your uploaded documents or any personal information within them.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information extracted from your resume/CV is used exclusively for the purpose of generating a score based on its content. This score is then presented to you for review. Once the extraction and scoring are completed, no data is saved or retained.

3. Data Security

We take your privacy and data security seriously. All data processing occurs in real-time, and no information is uploaded, stored, or saved on our servers. Your resume/CV text is processed temporarily and discarded immediately after scoring. We ensure that the entire process is secure and does not involve any permanent storage of your personal data.
